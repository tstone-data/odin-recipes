Authentic Street Tacos

Description

Easy, quick, authentic carne asada street tacos you can now make right at home!

Top with onion, cilantro + fresh lime juice! SO GOOD!

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbsp reduced sodium soy sauce
  • 2 Tbsp freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 2 Tbsp canola oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 1/2 lbs skirt steak
  • 12 mini flour tortillas
  • 3/4 cup diced red onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, combine soy sauce, lime juice, 1 tablespoon canola oil, garlic, chili powder, cumin and oregano

  2. In a gallon size Ziploc bag or large bowl, combine soy sauce mixture and steak; marinate for at least 1 hour up to 4 hours.

  3. Heat remaining 1 tablespoon canola oil in a large skillet over medium high heat.

    Add steak and marinade, and cook, stirring often, until steak has browned and marinade has reduced.

  4. Serve steak in tortillas, topped with onion, cilantro and lime.
